Email Design + Development

AMRMI Email Marketing

When I started at AMResorts our emails were completely image based, leading to a poor mobile experience and high bounce rate. Through working with our CRM team and Digital Designer I was able to develop a fully responsive email template for the Meetings & Incentvies segment.

Utilizing the web-based tool Litmus we were able ensure a enjoyable experience across every device and client imaginable. Litmus also provided us some additional analytics and tracking in addition to the ones we already had.
